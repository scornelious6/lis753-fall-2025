Eldest Daughter lyrics

The song reflects the effect of public and media criticism in Swift's life. A subtle nod to the "eldest daughter syndrome", it also revolves around the pressure and struggle to become a perfect individual before finally ending up with a happy relationship. (From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia)

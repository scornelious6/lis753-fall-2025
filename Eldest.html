The Fate of Ophelia lyrics

The song is partially inspired by Ophelia, a character who drowns due to madness from grief and romantic rejection in William Shakespeare's play Hamlet. It addresses an honorable soulmate who redeems Swift from a destiny of death like that of Ophelia. (From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia)
